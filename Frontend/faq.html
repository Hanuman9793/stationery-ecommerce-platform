Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, payments, and more.

Orders

Browse our shop, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your delivery details, choose your payment method (Cash on Delivery or UPI), and confirm your order. You'll receive an order reference number on screen.
Orders can be cancelled or modified before they are shipped. Please contact us immediately at inkandpaper97@gmail.com or call +91 98384 91752 with your order reference number.
You can track your order from the My Orders page after logging in. Alternatively, visit our Track Order page and enter your order reference number.
There is no minimum order value. However, orders below ₹100 will attract a delivery charge of ₹40. Orders of ₹100 and above enjoy free delivery within our delivery area.

Delivery

We currently deliver to selected areas around Renukoot Colony, Hindalco Colony, Renukoot, Uttar Pradesh – 231217. Orders outside our delivery zone may be cancelled and fully refunded. Contact us to confirm if your area is covered.
Standard delivery within our local area takes 1–2 business days. Same-day delivery may be available for select areas — contact us to check availability.
Yes! Delivery is FREE on all orders above ₹100. A flat ₹40 delivery charge applies to orders below ₹100.

Payments

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) and UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.). For UPI, transfer to marshal9793@oksbi and enter the 12-digit UTR reference number at checkout.
Yes. UPI payments are processed through your bank's secure infrastructure. We only collect a UTR reference number to verify the transaction — we never store card or bank details.
Please contact us at inkandpaper97@gmail.com with your UTR number and order reference. We'll verify and confirm your order manually within a few hours.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for items that are damaged, defective, or incorrectly shipped. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Visit our Returns page for full details.
Once your return is approved, refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. UPI refunds are instant after approval. COD refunds are issued via UPI transfer to your provided number.
