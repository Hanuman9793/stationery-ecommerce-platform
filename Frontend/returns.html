Returns & Refunds

Hassle-free returns within 7 days — your satisfaction is our priority.

01

Contact Us

Email or call us within 7 days of receiving your order with your order reference and reason.

02

We Review

Our team reviews your return request within 24 hours and confirms eligibility.

03

Refund Issued

Once approved, your refund is processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method.

Eligible Returns

We gladly accept returns if:

  • The item arrived damaged or defective
  • You received the wrong item
  • The item is significantly different from what was described
Returns must be requested within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in their original packaging.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items damaged due to misuse or mishandling by the customer
  • Items returned after the 7-day window
  • Items without original packaging
  • Perishable or consumable goods (e.g. ink refills once opened)
Change-of-mind returns are not accepted at this time. Please review your order carefully before confirming.

Refund Process

  • UPI Payments: Refunded to your original UPI ID within 1–3 business days after approval.
  • Cash on Delivery: Refunded via UPI transfer to a number you provide. Please share your UPI ID when raising the request.

You will be notified at each step of the refund process via the contact details you provided at checkout.

How to Raise a Return

To start a return, contact us with the following information:

  • Your order reference number (found in My Orders)
  • The item(s) you wish to return
  • A brief reason for the return
  • A photo of the damaged/incorrect item (if applicable)
