Delivery Area

At Soni Book Stall, we currently offer home delivery to select areas within our local zone in and around:

  • Renukoot Colony
  • Hindalco Colony
  • Renukoot, Uttar Pradesh – 231217
  • Surrounding localities (contact us to confirm)
Important: Orders placed from outside our delivery zone may be cancelled and refunded in full. Please contact us before ordering if you are unsure about your area.

Delivery Time

Standard Delivery

Orders are typically delivered within 1–2 business days of order confirmation. Business days are Monday through Saturday, 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM.

Same-Day Delivery

Same-day delivery may be available for select areas when ordered before 12:00 PM. Contact us at +91 98384 91752 to arrange same-day service.

Delays

Delivery times may be affected by local holidays, weather conditions, or high-demand periods. We will notify you if your order is delayed.

Order Processing

Once you place an order:

  • You receive an order reference number on-screen immediately.
  • Our team reviews and confirms your order within a few hours.
  • For UPI payments, we verify your UTR number before confirming.
  • Your order is packed and dispatched on the same or next business day.

You can track your order status from the My Orders page at any time.

Service Unavailable

If we are unable to fulfil your order due to being outside our delivery zone or any other reason, we will contact you within 24 hours. A full refund will be issued if payment was made in advance.
